NYU Child Language Lab Manual

7 Logistics

7.1 Lab Meetings 7

We hold lab meetings once a week during the semester and at the discretion of the PI during the summer. Lab meetings usually involve check-ins on recruiting and testinge fforts, data blitzes, presentations (lab internal and external) and feedback on projects. Location and time vary by semester and pandemic status.

7.2 Snacks [on hold during pandemic]

The lab manager is entrusted to get snacks for each lab meeting. The lab usually allots a budget of $20/wk for bagels and coffee. After buying said snacks, the lab manager is reimbursed from the PI’s research funds.

  1. Before the semester begins, the lab manager will contact the lab managers/PI of either lab spaces to check availability and block of a time slot that works best for lab members.↩︎
